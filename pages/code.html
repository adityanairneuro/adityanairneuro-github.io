The Claustrum Classifier

While at the Augustine Lab in Singapore, I created an online tool (based in R, using Shiny for GUIs) that automatically extracts electrophysiological data from files in the ABF format and applies a classifier trained to discern between 5 types of Claustrum neurons we determined using unsupervised methods

AdReconstructor and AdCount


Motivated by the need to have methods that extract morphological data from neuronal and subcellular structures, I wrote AdCount while at the National Neuroscience Institute, Singapore with Lim Kah Leong to allows for easy extraction of mitochondrial morphology from confocal images

During my undergraduate at the Karolinska Institute, I extended AdCount to enable automated reconstruction of neuronal morphology, to create AdReconstructor, based on the All-Path-Pruning algorithms written by the group of Hanchuan Peng at the Allen Institute for Brain Science
