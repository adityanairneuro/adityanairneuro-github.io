Over the past two years I studied the neural circuits of the Claustrum, a structure of the brain that's been hypothesized to control higher level cognition and possibly even consciousness!.

We made great strides in understanding the microcircuit of this structure and how neuromodulators regulate information flow through the claustrum at the lab of George Augustine at LKCMedicine, NTU

A Claustrum Cartograph

What kind of neurons exist in the claustrum and what does this diversity mean for the function of the claustrum? We studied this question in our paper published in eNeuro (Graf, Nair et al., 2020) and discovered a rich variety of claustrum neurons that differ in their intrinsic electrophysiological properties.



We even created a user-friendly software that allows researchers to look for these diverse neurons using machine-learning aided classification.
