Aditya Nair

A student looking for new avenues in neuroscience and computing.

I'm fascinated with neurons and all things neuroscience. Throughout my undergraduate journey, I have been fortunate to them these spiny biological structures at different levels; analyzing the molecules that make them, quantifying their morphology, simulating their function and most recently examining their electrical properties through electrophysiological methods. Throughout these experiences, I embraced programming to automate many features of conventional data analysis in neuroscience. This lead to the development of AdCount for the automated analysis of mitochondrial and lipid droplet morphology and SilberReconstructor, a hub for the automated reconstruction of 3D neuronal morphology. As I continue in my research, I hope to find more methods to marry computing and neuroscience as I try to uncover the function of different brain regions.
